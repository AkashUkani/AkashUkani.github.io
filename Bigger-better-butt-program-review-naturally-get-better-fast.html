Bigger Better Butt: Product Overview

Bigger Better Butt is a downloadable digital ebook which outlines how you can build a bigger butt through the right kind of exercises.

The 60 day exercise program prescribes 9 weeks worth of workouts, where you are working out 4 days of the week.

The beauty of this program is in its simplicity – there are only four exercises to learn, and the workouts follow the same format each week (more on this later). In short, this means you can focus fully on working your glutes out to the max, rather than trying to learn all sorts of complicated moves.

There’s not much information on nutrition in the Bigger Better Butt program, but it does come with a bonus ebook called “7 Tactics to Eat What You Want and Still Lose Weight” which talks a little bit about nutrition. However it doesn’t look like it’s specifically written to help with the whole Bigger Butt agenda which is a little bit of a shame.

The Workout

There are 9 weeks worth of workouts provided in the 60 day program. You will workout 4 days a week on Mondays, Tuesdays, Thursdays and Fridays (or you can shift these to fit with your schedule).

The good thing about this workout program is that there are only 2 exercises per workout, which keeps it nice and simple. There are usually single sets of high amounts of reps prescribed, which is different, to say the least! I think this must be the secret behind this program.

The workouts also seem to stay much the same from week to week, you just need to increase either weight or reps as specified. This is a good thing as it means you spend less time on trying to learn new moves, and more time on perfecting the prescribed exercises to get the most out of them.

One part that I struggled with was trying to work out starting weight and how much to increase weight by. This is where your skype sessions with Steve are ideal, as you can take these questions directly to him to get some personalized guidance.
